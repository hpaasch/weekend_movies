The Top 20 Movies of the entire history of film. Not really.

These films were evaluated by a cross-section of regular moviegoers, many of whom have rated a number of movies.

The results are weighted to focus on movies that at least 100 people rated, using that as a measure of intense interest. The first number after a movie title is the number of ratings it has. The average rating is then shown.

The movies are listed in descending order based on average rating, with the best movie at the top.

Each movie shows its average rating. See more on a movie by clicking on it.
